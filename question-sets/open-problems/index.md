## Status Definitions

- `raw`: Original problem is recorded, but mathematical objects are not fully specified.
- `theorem-draft`: Rewritten as background plus theorem form; some assumptions or parameters still need clarification.
- `scoped`: Objects, assumptions, target constants, or candidate counterexample spaces are clear.
- `active`: Proof work, numerical experiments, or counterexample construction is underway.
- `settled`: A clear conclusion or stage-level draft has been reached.
