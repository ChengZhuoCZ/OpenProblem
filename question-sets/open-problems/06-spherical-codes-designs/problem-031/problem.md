# Problem 031 - Small Shell and Rational Spherical Design Questions

## Source

This is taken from Eiichi Bannai's G2C2 Lecture No. 1 on spherical \(t\)-designs.

## Problem Statement

Resolve the following explicit existence questions from the lecture:

1. Is there a spherical \(6\)-design on a shell of a lattice \(L\subset\mathbb R^2\)?
2. Is there a spherical \(4\)-design on a shell of a lattice \(L\subset\mathbb R^3\)?
3. Is there a spherical \(4\)-design on \(S^1\) whose coordinates are all rational numbers?

Also investigate the analogous rational-coordinate question for larger \(t\).

## Background

These are concrete low-dimensional questions that sit between explicit design construction and arithmetic constraints on coordinates.

## Known Context

- The lecture points to rational designs and the Hilbert-Kampe problem.
- Lattice shells give natural arithmetic sources of spherical designs.
