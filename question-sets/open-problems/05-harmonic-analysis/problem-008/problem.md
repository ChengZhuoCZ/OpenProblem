# Problem 008 - Third-Order Generalized Additive Energy on Curves

## Source

This is Problem 3 from "Some Problems in Harmonic Analysis", contributed by Ciprian Demeter.

## Problem Statement

With \(E_n(A)\) denoting generalized additive energy, prove or disprove that, for every \(\varepsilon>0\),

\[
E_3(A)\lesssim_\varepsilon (\#A)^{3+\varepsilon}
\]

when \(A\subset S^1\) or \(A\subset P^1\), where \(P^1\) is the one-dimensional paraboloid/parabola model appearing in the source.

## Background

For \(S^1\), the estimate would follow from the unit distance conjecture. The source records an unconditional \(O((\#A)^{7/2})\) type bound from Szemeredi-Trotter methods.

## Known Context

- The problem links additive energy to incidence geometry.
- Decoupling gives the desired conclusion under polynomial separation assumptions.
